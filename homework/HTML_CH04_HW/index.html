My Hobby

One of my favorite hobbies to participate in is working out in the gym. Its my favorite because it challenges both my body and mind. I enjoy
pushing myself to lift heavier weights and improve my endurance. The gym provides a focused environment where i can unwind from the
stresses of life while staying committed to my health. I currently am doing a Push/Pull/Legs split.On push days I work out my chest,shoulders,
and triceps. On pull days I work out back and biceps. On leg days I workout my quads, hamstrings, glutes, and calves. On the next day rest
and I repeat the process the day after. Below is my workout routine:

Push

  • Bench Press:3 sets x 8 reps
  • Overhead Press Machine:3 sets x 12 reps
  • Dips:3 sets x 15 reps
  • EZ BarSkull Crusher(behind head):3 sets x 10 reps
  • Egyptian Cable Lateral Raise:3 sets x 12 reps
  • Cable Tricep Kick Back(single arm):3 sets x 20 reps

Pull

  • Wide Grip Pull Up:3 sets x 6 reps
  • Seated Cable Row(neutral grip):3 sets x 12 reps
  • Kneeling Cable Pullover:3 sets x 15 reps
  • Hammer Cheat Curls:3sets x 8 reps
  • Incline Dumbbell Curl:2 sets x 12 reps

Legs

  • Barbell Squat:3 sets x 6 reps
  • Barbell Romanian Deadlift:3 sets x 10 reps
  • Single Leg Press:3 sets x 15 reps
  • Legs Extension:3 sets x 10 reps
  • Seated Leg Curl Machine:3 sets x 12 reps
  • Calf Raise Machine:3 sets x 12 reps
