Grilled Cheese with Bacon

  • 6 Slices of Bacon
  • 4 SLices of Bread
  • 4 Slices of Cheddar Cheese
  • 2 Tablespoons of Mayonnaise
  1. Cook the Bacon: Fry the bacon in a pan until itâ€™s crispy. Remove the bacon and drain most of the fat, leaving about a tablespoon in the pan.
  2. Prepare the Bread: Spread mayonnaise on the outside of each slice of bread.
  3. Assemble the Sandwich Layer the cheese and bacon between two slices of bread, with the mayonnaise side facing out.
  4. Grill: Heat the pan over low heat. Place the sandwich in the pan and cook until golden brown on one side, then flip and cook until the cheese is melted and the other side is golden brown.
