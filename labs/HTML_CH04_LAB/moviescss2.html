Fantastic Mr.Fox

Fantastic Mr. Fox is a 2009 American animated adventure-comedy film directed by
Wes Anderson from a screenplay by Anderson and Noah Baumbach and based on the
novel of the same name by Roald Dahl. Featuring stop-motion animation. In the film,
a spree of thefts led by Mr. Fox results in his family, and later his community, being
hunted down by three farmers.

Staring

  • George Clooney
  • Meryl Streep
  • Jason Schwartzman
  • Bill Murray
  • Willem Dafoe
  • Owen Wilson.
